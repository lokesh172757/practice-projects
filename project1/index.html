A P J Abdul Kalam

1931 - 2015

Misile Man of INDIA

Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam was a great scientist and the 11th President of India. He was born on October 15, 1931, in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu. He played an important role in developing India’s missiles and space technology, which earned him the title "Missile Man of India." He also helped in making India a nuclear power. Later, as President, he became very popular, especially among students, and was known as the "People’s President." Dr. Kalam believed in hard work, honesty, and the power of education. He inspired millions with his simple life and great thoughts. He passed away on July 27, 2015, while giving a speech, doing what he loved most—teaching and inspiring young minds.
